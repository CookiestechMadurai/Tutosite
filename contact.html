Account Deletion Request

Request Account Deletion

Follow the Steps Below

To request the deletion of your account, please follow these steps:
  1. Send an email to tech.cookies1@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request."
  2. Include your username or email address associated with your account.
  3. Confirm that you want your account deleted.
Data Deletion

Upon request, we will delete the following data:

  • Personal information
  • Usage data
  • Any associated preferences
Retention Period

The following data may be retained for compliance purposes:

  • Any data required by law, retained for up to 12 months.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our support team.
